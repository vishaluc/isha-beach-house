Relax and Rejuvenate

The villa comes with 2 bedrooms with AC and with attached bathrooms plus a common bathroom, one living area, a functional kitchen and a large porch. It is fully furnished and able to accommodate up to 6-8 adults with extra bedding.

This place is well connected to some local eateries with authentic coastal cuisine of fish curry-rice. The vegetarians need not be disappointed as there are good vegetarian restaurants nearby as well.
